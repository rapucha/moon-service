Layer intent

  • NearHouses, trees, fastest drift, strongest opacity.
  • MidChurch and mid-rise forms, medium drift, quieter opacity.
  • FarLow hills and sparse towers, slowest drift, faintest opacity.

The layer speeds are intentionally not tied to the hour axis. Under reduced motion, all layers freeze into a calm static frame.
