Sun And Moon Sky Position Mockups

These variants keep the Moon pass timeline readable while adding a true azimuth/altitude view for the Sun and Moon. Example facts use Prague, 9 July 2026, 15:06 local time.

Option A

Timeline plus sky-position inset

The timeline remains Moon-first. A compact quasi-3D dome shows the actual Sun and Moon positions at the suggested time.

Suggested 15:06 Moon WNW 6 deg Sun SW 54 deg 68 deg apart

Option B

Stacked Sun context row plus larger sky dome

The Sun becomes a separate context track under the Moon timeline. The dome owns all azimuth comparison.

Moon path Sun altitude context Sky geometry separate

Option C

Pass sky-track map with timeline facts

A full-pass azimuth/altitude map shows the Moon and Sun tracks as different paths. This is more explicit, but heavier than a single-moment inset.
